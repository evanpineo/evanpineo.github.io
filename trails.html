Trail Conditions

The listing of trails below are my favorite trails to ride around Cincinnati. Devou Park is one of the most enjoyable because I live close to it and has a very "flowy" feel to it as well as some very aggressive downhill sections. Eastfork, England Idlewild, and Mitchell Memorial are all very solid trails to ride as they have very balanced sections of climbing and descending and are well marked.
